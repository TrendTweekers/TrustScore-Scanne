Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

TrustScore Scanner is a Shopify app that analyzes store trust signals and provides AI-powered audit recommendations. This privacy policy explains how we handle data.

Data We Collect

TrustScore Scanner collects:

  • Store Information: Your Shopify store URL, app installation date, and subscription plan
  • Public Store Content: Screenshots of your public storefront pages (homepage, product pages, checkout page) for security auditing
  • Audit Results: Trust scores, AI analysis, and recommendations generated from your store audits
  • Store Metadata: Product count, theme information (from public APIs)

Data We DO NOT Collect

We explicitly DO NOT access or store:

  • Customer data (names, emails, payment info, order history)
  • Product descriptions or inventory details
  • Customer transactions or order information
  • Sensitive admin data
  • Any private store backend data

How We Use Data

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Generate trust audit scores and analysis for your store
  • Track your store's trust improvements over time
  • Send weekly monitoring reports (PRO/PLUS only)
  • Provide competitor trust benchmarking (PLUS only)
  • Improve AI analysis accuracy via anonymized aggregates

Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Role-based access controls (only authenticated merchants)
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits
  • No third-party data sharing

Data Retention

We retain:

  • Store records: Active until app is uninstalled
  • Scan history: All historical audits for trend tracking
  • Screenshots: Latest screenshot from each scan (older ones deleted)

When you uninstall the app, all associated data is marked as inactive. When you request data deletion (via GDPR shop/redact), all data is permanently deleted.

GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR and Shopify's data handling requirements:

  • Data Subject Requests: Respond within 30 days
  • Right to Deletion: All shop data deleted on request via shop/redact webhook
  • Data Portability: Export your audit history via the app dashboard
  • Data Processing: Act as a data processor on behalf of your store

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Shopify APIs: To authenticate and access public store data
  • Anthropic Claude AI: For trust analysis and recommendations (does not train on your data)
  • Railway (Heroku-like): For app hosting and data storage

All third-party services comply with data protection regulations.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we store about your store
  • Request deletion of your store data
  • Export your audit history
  • Uninstall the app at any time

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify merchants of material changes via email or in-app notification.
